What This Does

Brand Decoder is a strategic analysis tool. Enter any company name and it maps out how the brand presents itself — visually and verbally — then identifies the white space a challenger or evolved version of that brand could own.

How to Use It Well
01 Type a brand name — any company, big or small. The more well-known, the richer the analysis.
02 Use the optional context field to sharpen the lens — add a competitor, an industry, or the angle you care about most.
03 Read the White Space section first — that's the gap. Everything else is the evidence behind it.
04 Use the Save button to export a plain-text report you can paste into a brief, deck, or strategy doc.
